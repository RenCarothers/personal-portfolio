Web Game Reboot is a Unity WebGL interactive, visual novel style videogame, designed for my solo Ada capstone culminating project. The user is invited to reimagine a day in the life of junior high student, Rockett on their first day at a new school. Wherein by making difficult choices regarding a tenious school bully, the user is provided an individuated outcome to Rockett's day. Created with Unity & C#. [Demo | Repo]

Online store Neopetsy features a dual interface account management system for merchants and shoppers, implementing OAuth with Github. Following an agile design framework, a team of three colleagues and I developed this Ruby on Rails mock Etsy site in a two week sprint. Sellers can manage their orders and inventory via a merchant dashboard, whilst shoppers can rate and review items and utilize our built-in webcart checkout experience. I developed the merchant dashboard front/backend, as well as the overall front end web design and styles. [Demo | Repo]

Rideshare app Ride the Rails provides a fictious community with easy CRUD management of public records regarding local drivers and passengers. Customers can request rides to be matched with available drivers. New drivers can sign up to be added to the db queue. Ride The Rails was pair programmed with Ruby on Rails by me and a fellow Adie. I specialized in the restful routing whilst my partner honed in on unit testing our project. [Demo | Repo]
